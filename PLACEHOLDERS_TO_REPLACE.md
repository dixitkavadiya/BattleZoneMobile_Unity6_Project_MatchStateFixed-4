# BattleZone Mobile - Placeholder Replacement List

This list separates current temporary content from production-ready targets. Nothing in this project uses copied BGMI/PUBG assets, but most visuals are still original placeholders or visual proxies.

## Needs Professional Character Artists

- Runtime player humanoid generated by `BattleZoneRuntimeBuilder`.
- Runtime bot humanoids.
- Final tactical operator mesh, UVs, textures, rig, skinning, and animation clips.
- Character customization, face/helmet variants, gloves, boots, armor pieces, and backpack variants.

## Needs Professional Weapon Artists

- Runtime weapon models for all weapons.
- Visual-slice proxy weapons `VS_Rook17_AssaultRifle_Showcase` and `VS_Sable9_Pistol_Showcase`.
- Final assault rifle and pistol meshes, textures, animations, attachment meshes, muzzle/socket placement, and reload hand alignment.
- Remaining weapon families: SMG, sniper, shotgun, melee.

## Needs Professional Environment Artists

- Runtime terrain/world blocks.
- Existing procedural city, village, forest, base, factory, coast, roads, rocks, trees, props, and interiors.
- Milestone 21 playable island terrain, village houses, warehouse district, shipping container yard, military checkpoint, gas station, school, hospital, forest, hills, river, bridges, roads, interiors, rooftop access, loot anchors, runtime NavMesh helpers, and reflection probes are generated proxy content, not final authored production art.
- Milestone 20 Unity Terrain heightmap, terrain-layer textures, detail grass, houses, town, military base, warehouse, shipping yard, gas station, river, bridges, weather, clouds, rain particles, probes, and optimization markers are generated proxy content, not final authored AAA environment art.
- Milestone 19 drop terrain, village, forest, river, hills, roads, bridge, container yard, military camp, parachute canopy, and landing cues.
- Visual-slice street block, enterable house, warehouse, vegetation, rocks, and loot crate are art-direction proxies, not final authored assets.
- Milestone 17 district, apartment, warehouse, shop, guard tower, street props, terrain strips, river edge, vegetation, and lighting props are custom generated vertical-slice proxies, not final authored FBX/texture assets.
- Final modular kits: roads, sidewalks, curbs, buildings, interiors, stairs, windows, doors, fences, props, cliffs, riverbanks, vegetation clusters, decals, and cover pieces.

## Needs Professional Vehicle Artists

- Runtime jeep, buggy, motorcycle, pickup, and aircraft proxies.
- Final vehicle meshes, interiors, wheel/suspension pieces, damage states, glass, lights, and texture atlases.

## Needs Professional UI And Audio

- Runtime UI is functional but still prototype-level for final product polish.
- Procedural temporary gun sounds, footsteps, ambient loops, UI clicks, and zone warning sounds need final original audio design.
- Final iconography, HUD art, inventory item icons, minimap styling, and settings visuals still need design/art passes.

## Artist-Ready But Not Production-Ready

- `Assets/BattleZoneMobile/ArtPipeline/PrefabSlots`
- `Assets/BattleZoneMobile/ArtPipeline/VisualDefinitions`
- `BZ_Main` visual-slice showcase objects

These are intended to make replacement safe and repeatable. They are not final shipped art.
